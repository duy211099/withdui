# Introduction to the Concept of Autonomy

- Autonomy is the freedom to have control over one's work and decisions.
- Autonomy can significantly impace motivation and satisfaction
- Giving more control leads to higher engagement, creativity, and productivity. Eg: allowing to choos how to complete a task can result in innovative solutions and a greater sense of ownership.

# The Benifits of Autonomy

- When employees feel autonomous, it extends beyond individual satisfaction and contributes to organizational success
- Increased engagement, better performance, and reduced turnover are some of the key benefits of autonomy. For instance, an employee who can decide how to structure their workday may find more efficient ways to complete tasks, leading to higher productivity and job satisfaction.

# Practical Ways to Enhance Autonomy for Employees

- Provide Choices
- Set Clear Goals but Allow Flexibility
- Encourage Self-Management
